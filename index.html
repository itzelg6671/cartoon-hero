About Groot

Groot is a extraterrestrial tree-like creature who is a founding member of Star-Lord's Guardians of the Galaxy.

Groot and the Guardians of the Galaxy

Groot's Characteristics

  1. Ability to create multiple miniature copies of himself known as cuttings which retain all his memories
  2. Can speak through trees of all forms
  3. Has super strength
  4. Genius-level intellect
